Entres

1. Grilled Salmon with Spinach Salad

  • Salmon, fresh spinach, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and a lemon vinaigrette.

2. Chicken Stir-Fry with Broccoli and Garlic

  • Skinless chicken breast, broccoli, bell peppers, garlic, and ginger, served over brown rice.

3. Beef and Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

  • Lean ground beef, quinoa, black beans, diced tomatoes, and spices, all stuffed into bell peppers.

4. Turmeric Chicken with Cauliflower Rice

  • Chicken thighs marinated in turmeric, served with sautéed cauliflower rice and mixed vegetables.

5. Pork Tenderloin with Brussels Sprouts

  • Roasted pork tenderloin, Brussels sprouts, and a side of sweet potatoes.

6. Lentil and Chicken Soup

  • Shredded chicken, lentils, carrots, celery, and herbs simmered in vegetable broth.

7. Sardine and Avocado Toast

  • Whole grain bread topped with canned sardines, smashed avocado, and a sprinkle of lemon juice.

8. Baked Chicken with Asparagus and Nuts

  • Baked chicken breast served with roasted asparagus and a sprinkle of chopped walnuts.

9. Ground Turkey and Vegetable Skillet

  • Ground turkey, zucchini, bell peppers, and spinach sautéed together with spices.

10. Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw

  • Grilled white fish (like tilapia), topped with a cabbage slaw made from red cabbage, carrots, and a lime dressing.

Vegan Entres

1. Quinoa Salad with Spinach and Avocado

  • Quinoa, fresh spinach, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and a lemon-tahini dressing.

2. Lentil and Vegetable Stir-Fry

  • Green or brown lentils, broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and garlic sautéed in olive oil with ginger and soy sauce.

3. Chickpea and Sweet Potato Curry

  • Chickpeas, sweet potatoes, coconut milk, spinach, and spices (curry powder, turmeric) served over brown rice.

4. Stuffed Bell Peppers with Brown Rice and Black Beans

  • Bell peppers stuffed with a mixture of brown rice, black beans, corn, tomatoes, and spices, then baked until tender.

5. Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic Glaze

  • Brussels sprouts roasted with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, served with toasted walnuts and cranberries.

6. Cauliflower Tacos with Cabbage Slaw

  • Roasted cauliflower florets seasoned with spices, served in corn tortillas and topped with a slaw made from red cabbage, carrots, and lime dressing.

Desserts

1. Chia Seed Pudding with Berries

  • Chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and a mix of fresh berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries).

2. Avocado Chocolate Mousse

  • Ripe avocados, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.

3. Oatmeal Cookies with Nuts and Dark Chocolate

  • Rolled oats, almond flour, chopped walnuts or almonds, dark chocolate chips, banana (mashed), and a dash of cinnamon.

4. Fruit Salad with Citrus Dressing

  • A variety of fresh fruits (such as apples, oranges, kiwi, and pomegranate), fresh mint, and a dressing made from orange juice, lemon juice, and a drizzle of honey.

5. Turmeric Coconut Energy Balls

  • Rolled oats, almond butter, shredded coconut, honey or maple syrup, and turmeric powder.

Vegan Desserts

1. Coconut Mango Chia Pudding

  • Chia seeds, coconut milk, ripe mango (diced), maple syrup (optional), fresh mint (for garnish).

2. Sweet Potato Brownies

  • Cooked sweet potatoes (mashed), almond flour, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.

3. Berry Quinoa Salad

  • Cooked quinoa, mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries), lime juice, chopped mint, and a drizzle of agave syrup (optional).
