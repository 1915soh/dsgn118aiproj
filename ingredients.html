Ingredients

We source all of our ingredients from small farmers. The following are some of the ingredients we source to bring you the best meals! We are always happy to accomodate for subsitutions and allergies. Please feel free to let your server know if you would like to make any changes!

1. Leafy Greens

  • Spinach, kale, collard greens.
  • Benefits: High in antioxidants and chlorophyll, which can help detoxify and reduce the burden of toxins.

2. Cruciferous Vegetables

  • Broccoli, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts.
  • Benefits: Contain compounds that enhance detoxification enzymes and help reduce fat.

3. Berries

  • Blueberries, strawberries, raspberries.
  • Benefits: Rich in antioxidants and vitamins, which help protect from damage and inflammation.

4. Nuts and Seeds

  • Walnuts, almonds, flaxseeds, chia seeds.
  • Benefits: High in healthy fats, omega-3 fatty acids, and antioxidants that support function and reduce inflammation.

5. Avocado

  • Benefits: Contains healthy fats and compounds that may help produce glutathione, a powerful antioxidant that aids in detoxification.

6. Garlic

  • Benefits: Contains allicin and selenium, which help detoxify and reduce inflammation.

7. Olive Oil

  • Benefits: A healthy fat that can reduce bad enzyme levels and improve insulin sensitivity, promoting health.

8. Turmeric

  • Benefits: Contains curcumin, which has anti-inflammatory properties and may help reduce damage.

9. Green Tea

  • Benefits: Rich in antioxidants like catechins, which can improve body function and reduce fat accumulation.

10. Whole Grains

  • Brown rice, quinoa, oats.
  • Benefits: High in fiber, which helps regulate blood sugar levels and supports overall metabolic health.

11. Legumes

  • Lentils, chickpeas, beans.
  • Benefits: High in fiber and protein, helping to maintain healthy blood sugar levels and support body function.

12. Fruits

  • Apples, citrus fruits, grapes.
  • Benefits: High in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that help detoxify and repair.
